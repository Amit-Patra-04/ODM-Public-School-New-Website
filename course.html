Our Courses

Cources We Offer

ODM Public School offers a diverse range of courses designed to cater to the academic and developmental needs of students from Nursery to Class 12. The school follows the CBSE curriculum, providing a strong foundation in subjects like Science, Commerce, Humanities, and Technology. Alongside core academics, ODM emphasizes skill-based learning through various elective courses, vocational programs, and co-curricular activities, ensuring a balanced education that prepares students for competitive exams and future careers.

Nursury - Matriculation

ODM Public School offers a comprehensive CBSE curriculum from Nursery to Class 10, focusing on core subjects like Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Languages. The school integrates co-curricular activities, skill development, and modern teaching methods to ensure a balanced and holistic education for every student.

+2 Science

ODM Public School's +2 Science program, following the CBSE curriculum, offers core subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology, along with Computer Science and Physical Education. Students can also choose a 6th optional subject, with Painting and Odissi available, fostering both academic and creative growth for a well-rounded education.

+2 Commerce

ODM Public School's +2 Commerce program follows the CBSE curriculum, offering subjects such as Accountancy, Business Studies, Economics, and Mathematics. The program emphasizes practical learning and skill development, preparing students for careers in business, finance, and entrepreneurship.
