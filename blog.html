Our Campus Programs

We create the signs of commitment, dedication and hope within your child to carry the human excellence ahead.


ODM aims at the holistic development of its students. Thus, we not only provide quality education but also offer the most interesting and out-of-the-box activities for students to acknowledge their hidden talents and for us to provide the right exposure and platform to that talent. We lay due emphasis on different activities through active participation in various social, athletic, and artistic clubs and societies. We guide students to discover and create their own understanding.


Hostel accommodation is available to the students after admission in class - XI/XII (Residential plan). School has several separate residential houses within the school campus as well as in the adjacent area. Girls and Boys reside in separate Boarding house. The Boarders live in the comfort of luxurious and centrally air-conditioned boarding rooms.


Forging resilient young men into powerful revolution-bringers with respect, responsibility, compassion and honesty.
